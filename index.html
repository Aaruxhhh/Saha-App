Help & Support

How do bookings work?expand_more

Browse helpers by category, select one, pick a date & time, and confirm. The payment is held in escrow until the job is completed.

How does payment work?expand_more

Payments are secured through our escrow system. Money is only released to the helper after you confirm the job is done.

How do I cancel a booking?expand_more

Go to Bookings → tap on the booking → Cancel. Cancellations within 1 hour of booking are free. After that, a 10% fee may apply.

How do I become a helper?expand_more

Switch to "Earning" mode from bookings, complete your KYC verification, and set up your services and pricing.
